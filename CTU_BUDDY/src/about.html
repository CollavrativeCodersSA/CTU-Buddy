About Us

Our Story

Welcome to CTU Buddy. This is a tool for students to stay organized and succeed academically. Our mission is to make your college life easier by providing a more efficient timetable tailored to the unique demands of your classes.

As former students ourselves, we also struggled to keep track of our classes, assessments, part-time jobs, and social life. So we created CTU Buddy to help students manage their time better.

Meet the Team

Each of our team members brings unique styles and skills that contribute to making CTU Buddy a success. We support you every step of the way.
